Our Story

Jami and I first met in the Fall of 2019 at BYU-Idaho at an opening ward social. While we didn’t start dating for a few weeks, we quickly became friends, bonding over a shared love of baked goods and board games. We had our first date a few weeks after meeting at nature park where hours and conversation topics passed by without us realizing. From there we spent the next year dating and enjoying each other’s company as we went forward in our collective studies. It was about a year into dating each other that Jami decided to go and serve a mission, so in January of 2021 she did just that serving in the Iowa City mission. Jami and I wrote to each other throughout her mission, sharing stories and encouraging each other until we were reunited this last summer. From there it seemed like no time had passed as we talked and dated, rejoicing in seeing each other again. Finally, after almost 3 years of dating, I asked Jami to Marry me in the same spot that we had our first date, thankfully, as evident by the existence of this website, she said yes. We are now planning on getting married on December 29th this year in Gilbert, Arizona (for more information see the wedding details page).
